Meet Nutishia

Nutishia is a Graphic Designer and Full Stack Developer in Durham, North Carolina. Art and design have always been a passion for her. Being both driven and self-motivated, she is constantly experimenting with new technologies and techniques as she strives to better herself as a developer.

Nutishia possesses experience in both front and back-end development, and is proficient in HTML/HTML5, CSS/CSS3, JavaScript, jQuery, NodeJS and AJAX to name a few. She utilizes Git version control and likes to work with the Bootstrap framework. She also possesses great skill with Adobe Illustrator, Photoshop, and InDesign. Nutishia is devoted to modern and clean designs and am an advocate of structure and simplicity in both design and code.
