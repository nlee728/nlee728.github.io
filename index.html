ABOUT ME

I am a Full Stack Developer and Graphic Designer in Durham, North Carolina.

I have been creating art for as long as I can remember. From my early days of sketching and painting and then with vector design and web development, I am always ready to tackle the next project.

I love all things Adobe and am a React fanatic. I have designed websites, apps and logos for organizations like the Duke University School of Medicine Zebrafish Core Facilities, Elevation Properties, Lott Realty and many more. I have also had my work featured in scientific journals such as Developmental Cell and Current Biology.

Recent Development Projects

YumBot - A Personal Digital Cookbook

YumBot allows the user to create an inventory of ingredients on hand, then submit a search to the Recipe Puppy API to return recipes that include one or more of those ingredients. Users can favorite recipes to have them saved to their own Favorite Recipes Page. They can also save and edit their own recipes via their own Personal Recipes Page. Once saved, the recipes can be printed, emailed or tweeted.

FishTrak - Zebrafish Management System

The FishTrak app allows research labs to keep track of their stocks in a digital format accessible to everyone within the lab. It also allows husbandry staff to communicate more easily with the research staff regarding the fish in their care.

Clicky Game - Javascript Game

A game where players have to click on every image once to win the game. Click on any twice and it's game over.

Pick A Flick - Movie Selection Application

Users are able to create a group and vote with other users in the group on movies available in their zipcode. The TMS API will choose 5 movies and once the users submit their votes, the Open Agora API will tally the votes and show the winning movie. Users will then be able to vote on showtimes for their chosen movie.
