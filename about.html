About Nutishia

Hi! My name is Nutishia and I am a Graphic Designer and Full Stack Developer in Durham, North Carolina. Art and design have always been a passion for me. Being both driven and self-motivated, I am constantly experimenting with new technologies and techniques as I strive to better myself as a developer.

I possess experience in both front and back-end development, and I am proficient in HTML/HTML5, CSS/CSS3, JavaScript, jQuery, NodeJS and AJAX to name a few. I utilize Git version control and like to work with the Bootstrap framework. I also possess great skill with Adobe Illustrator, Photoshop, and InDesign. I'm devoted to modern and clean designs and am an advocate of structure and simplicity in both design and code.
